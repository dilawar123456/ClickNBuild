Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new project?

To create a new project, go to the "Create Project" page and fill in all the required details about your project. Make sure to provide clear specifications and requirements.

How can I track my project's progress?

You can track your project's progress by visiting the "My Projects" page. Each project will show its current status and any updates from the assigned builder.

What should I do if I have an issue with a builder?

If you encounter any issues with a builder, please contact our support team immediately. We'll help resolve the matter and ensure your project continues smoothly.
